Bio

I am a Master of Science student in the Regional Science program at Cornell University, focusing on data-driven approaches for sustainable tranportation and mobility systems. Currently, I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. H. Oliver Gao at the Lab. Additionally, I am employed as a lab assistant in the Department of Information Science and serve as a research assistant under the supervision of Prof. Andreas Malikopoulos at the Information and Decision Science Laboratory.

Before coming to Cornell, I received my BS in Business Intelligence and Data Analytics at the University of Macau. In its interim, I spent a semester on exchange in Xi'an Jiaotong University's Big Data Management and Application program at the School of Management. During the summer of 2022, I had the opportunity to intern at Bayer as a developer. My responsibilities included developing data analysis software using R and Python. I was also responsible for machine-learning projects to fit pharmacokinetic parameters by learning medical images.
