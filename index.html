Bio

I am a Master of Science student in the Regional Science program at Cornell University, focusing on spatial data analytics and computational methods for the system engineering field. Currently, I am fortunate enough to be advised by Oliver Gao. Additionally, I am employed as a lab assistant in the Department of Information Science and serve as a research assistant under the supervision of Andreas Malikopoulos at the Information and Decision Science Lab.

Before coming to Cornell, I received my BS in Business Intelligence and Data Analytics at the University of Macau with a top 5% GPA. In its interim, I spent a semester on exchange in Xi'an Jiaotong University's Big Data Management and Applications program at the School of Management. During the summer of 2022, I had the opportunity to intern at Bayer as a developer intern. My responsibilities included developing data analysis software using R and Python. I was also responsible for machine-learning projects to fit pharmacokinetic parameters by learning medical images.
