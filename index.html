Bio

I am a Master of Science student in the Regional Science program at Cornell University, focusing on spatial data analytics and machine learning methods aiding system enegineering field. Currently, I am fortunate enough to be advised by Oliver Gao , works as a lab assistant in the Department of Information Science, and works as a research assistant under Andreas Malikopoulos at the Information and Decision Science Lab.

Prior to coming Cornell, I received my Bachelor of Science degree in Business Intelligence and Data Analytics at the University of Macau with a top 5% GPA. In its interim, I spent a semester on exchange in Xi'an Jiaotong University's Big Data Management and Applications program at the School of Management. In 2022 summer, I interned at Bayer as a developer intern responsible for developing data analysis software in R and Python. I was also responsible for machine-learning projects to fit pharmacokinetic parameters by learning medical images. I also assisted in the maintenance of the cloud platform and in collecting and organizing medical papers.
