Bio

I am a first-year PhD student in the Arndt-Corden Department of Economics at the Australian National University. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Yu Sheng and Dr. Hayley Henderson at the Crawford School of Public Policy.

Before coming to ANU, I received my MS in Regional Science from Cornell University and my BS in Business Intelligence and Data Analytics from the University of Macau.
